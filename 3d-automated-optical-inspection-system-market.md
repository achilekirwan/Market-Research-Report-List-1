<p><p>The 3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) system market is divided primarily into two types: Inline 3D AOI and Offline 3D AOI. Inline 3D AOI systems are integrated into the manufacturing line, allowing for real-time inspection of products as they are produced, ensuring immediate quality control. In contrast, Offline 3D AOI systems operate independently of the production line, enabling batch inspection of finished products. Both types enhance defect detection and improve overall product quality in various manufacturing processes.</p></p>
