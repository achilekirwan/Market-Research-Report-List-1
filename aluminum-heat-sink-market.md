<p><strong>The Aluminum Heat Sink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Heat Sink</li><li>Active Heat Sink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum heat sink market is divided into two main types: passive and active heat sinks. Passive heat sinks rely on natural convection and radiation to dissipate heat, utilizing their large surface area for effective thermal management without additional energy input. Conversely, active heat sinks incorporate mechanical devices, such as fans or pumps, to enhance cooling efficiency by actively circulating air or liquid, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Both types are crucial for maintaining optimal operating temperatures in electronic devices.</p></p>
