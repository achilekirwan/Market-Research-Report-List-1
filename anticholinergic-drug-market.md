<p><p>The anticholinergic drug market is categorized into three main types based on administration routes: oral, parenteral, and topical. Parenteral formulations, delivered via injections, offer rapid action for acute situations, such as anesthesia or severe allergies. Each type caters to specific therapeutic needs and patient preferences in treatment regimens.</p></p>
