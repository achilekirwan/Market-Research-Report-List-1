<p><ul><li>Inductive Sensor Type</li><li>Hall Sensor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive ignition module market can be segmented into two main types: inductive sensor type and Hall sensor type. Inductive sensor types utilize a coil to generate a voltage in response to changing magnetic fields, providing reliable ignition timing. In contrast, Hall sensor types employ magnetism to detect the position of the crankshaft or camshaft, offering precise timing control with minimal maintenance needs. Both technologies are vital for enhancing engine performance and efficiency in modern vehicles.</p></p>
