<p><p>The battery-type washing machine market includes three main types: push-type scrubbers, ride-on scrubbers, and rear-standing scrubbers. Push-type scrubbers are manually operated, allowing users to control the machine while walking behind it, ideal for smaller areas. Ride-on scrubbers are larger, enabling operators to sit while cleaning, making them suitable for extensive spaces like warehouses. Rear-standing scrubbers feature a design where the user stands at the rear while maneuvering, offering a balance between control and coverage for medium-sized environments.</p></p>
