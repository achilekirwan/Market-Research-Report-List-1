<p><p>The carbon fiber resin market is primarily segmented into two types: PAN-based carbon fiber and pitch-based carbon fiber. PAN-based carbon fiber, derived from polyacrylonitrile, is known for its high strength, low weight, and excellent durability, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, pitch-based carbon fiber is produced from petroleum pitches, offering high thermal and electrical conductivity, suited for industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse sectors, driving innovation and advancements in composite materials.</p></p>
