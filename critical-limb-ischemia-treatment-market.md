<p><p>Critical Limb Ischemia (CLI) is a severe obstruction of blood flow to the extremities, often resulting in pain, non-healing wounds, and potentially leading to amputation if left untreated. Treatment options for CLI include revascularization procedures, such as angioplasty and bypass surgery, pharmacological therapies to manage symptoms and improve circulation, and wound care management.
