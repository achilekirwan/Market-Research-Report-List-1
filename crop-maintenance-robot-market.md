<p><ul><li>Unmanned Ground Robots (UGR)</li><li>Unmanned Aerial Robots (UAR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Crop Maintenance Robot market is divided into two main types: Unmanned Ground Robots (UGR) and Unmanned Aerial Robots (UAR). UGRs operate on the ground, performing tasks such as planting, weeding, and monitoring soil conditions, thus enhancing efficiency in crop management. In contrast, UARs utilize aerial capabilities to conduct surveillance, assess crop health, and apply pesticides or fertilizers from above, providing a broader view of agricultural fields. Together, these technologies optimize farm productivity and resource management.</p></p>
