<p><strong>The Doppler Flow Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-sensor Doppler Flow Meters</li><li>Two-sensor Doppler Flow Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Doppler flow meters are divided into one-sensor and two-sensor types based on their operating principle. One-sensor Doppler flow meters utilize a single sensor to emit ultrasonic waves and analyze the frequency shift of reflected waves, providing flow velocity measurements. In contrast, two-sensor Doppler flow meters use two sensors positioned at different points to capture and compare frequency shifts, allowing for more accurate flow rate calculations. Both types are widely applied in various industries for liquid flow measurement.</p></p>
