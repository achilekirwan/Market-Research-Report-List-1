<p><ul><li>SPD</li><li>PDLC</li><li>Electrochromic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dynamic glass market comprises three main types: Suspended Particle Display (SPD), Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC), and Electrochromic glass. SPD utilizes suspended particles in a liquid, allowing for instant tint adjustment for privacy and glare control. PDLC features liquid crystals that switch from clear to opaque when an electric current is applied, offering privacy and light control. Electrochromic glass changes tint in response to electrical signals, enabling energy efficiency and glare reduction while enhancing aesthetic appeal. Each type serves unique architectural and functional needs.</p></p>
