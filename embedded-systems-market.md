<p><p>The embedded systems market comprises two primary segments: embedded hardware and embedded software. Embedded hardware includes microcontrollers, sensors, and application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs) that facilitate the functioning of electronic devices. In contrast, embedded software encompasses the operating systems, firmware, and application programs that control the hardware and enable specific functionalities. Together, these components drive innovation in various industries, including automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics, enhancing device performance, efficiency, and user experience.</p></p>
