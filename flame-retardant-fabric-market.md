<p><p>The flame retardant fabric market comprises two primary types: inherent flame retardant fabrics and treated flame retardant fabrics. Inherent flame retardant fabrics are made from fibers that possess flame-resistant properties intrinsically, providing lasting protection without additional treatments. In contrast, treated flame retardant fabrics start with regular materials that undergo chemical treatment to enhance their flame resistance. Both types are used across various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and construction, ensuring safety and compliance with fire regulations.</p></p>
