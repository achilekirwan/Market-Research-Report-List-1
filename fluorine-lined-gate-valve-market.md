<p><p>The fluorine lined gate valve market comprises three primary types: pneumatic, electric, and manual. Pneumatic gate valves utilize compressed air for operation, offering quick response and automation, ideal for high-frequency applications. Electric gate valves are powered by electric actuators, providing precise control and remote operation, making them suitable for complex processes. Manual gate valves rely on direct human operation through a handwheel, offering simplicity and reliability for less frequent use. Each type caters to specific industrial requirements and operational needs.</p></p>
