<p><ul><li>Serum-Free</li><li>With Serum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Freezing media is essential for preserving cells during cryopreservation. The market is categorized into two main types: serum-free and with serum. Serum-free media eliminates variability and potential contamination from animal-derived components, making it ideal for research and regenerative medicine. In contrast, media with serum, often supplemented with bovine serum, provides essential growth factors and proteins that enhance cell viability and growth, particularly for established cell lines. The choice between the two depends on specific cell requirements and experimental goals.</p></p>
