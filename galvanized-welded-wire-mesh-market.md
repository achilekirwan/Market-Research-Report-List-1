<p><ul><li>Hot-dip Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh</li><li>Cold Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized welded wire mesh market is categorized into two primary types: hot-dip galvanized welded wire mesh and cold galvanized welded wire mesh. Hot-dip galvanization involves immersing welded wire in molten zinc, providing robust corrosion resistance and durability, ideal for outdoor applications. In contrast, cold galvanization applies a zinc coating through electroplating, resulting in a thinner layer, suitable for less demanding environments. Both types serve diverse applications, including construction, agriculture, and security, catering to varying needs based on strength and longevity.</p></p>
