<p><p>The insecticides market includes several key types. Pyrethroids are synthetic compounds mimicking natural pyrethrins, known for their effectiveness against a wide range of pests. Organophosphorus insecticides disrupt the nervous system of insects and are widely used in agriculture. Carbamates function similarly but are less persistent in the environment. Organochlorines, though effective, face regulatory scrutiny due to environmental persistence. Botanicals, derived from plants, offer a natural alternative, appealing to organic farming and environmentally conscious consumers, promoting sustainable pest control solutions.</p></p>
