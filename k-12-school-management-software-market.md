<p><p>The K-12 school management software market is primarily divided into two types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed locally on school servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on external servers, offering scalability, remote access, and automatic updates with lower upfront costs. Schools often choose between these options based on their specific needs, budget, and IT capabilities.</p></p>
