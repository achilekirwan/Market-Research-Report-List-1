<p><p>The lubricating paste market primarily consists of two main types: Barium Nitride (BN) and Molybdenum Disulfide (MoS2). BN is known for its exceptional thermal stability and electrical insulation properties, making it ideal for high-temperature applications. In contrast, MoS2 offers excellent lubricative properties, reducing friction in various mechanical systems, especially under extreme conditions. Both types are essential in industries like automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, providing reliable performance and extended life for machinery and components.</p></p>
