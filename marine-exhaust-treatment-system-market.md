<p><p>The Marine Exhaust Treatment System market includes several types designed to reduce emissions from marine engines. Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Systems recirculate a portion of the engine's exhaust back to the combustion chamber to lower nitrogen oxide emissions. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Systems utilize a catalyst and a reductant, typically urea, to convert harmful nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen and water. Other systems encompass various technologies such as particulate filters and marine scrubbers, tailored to meet environmental regulations.</p></p>
