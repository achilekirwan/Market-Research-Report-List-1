<p><p>The O-ring gripper market comprises primarily two types: parallel grippers and angular grippers. Parallel grippers operate by moving two fingers directly toward or away from each other, ensuring even gripping force and stability, which suits tasks requiring precision. Angular grippers, on the other hand, function by rotating the fingers to grasp objects, offering versatility in accessing confined spaces. Both types find applications across various industries, enhancing automation and efficiency in material handling and assembly processes.</p></p>
