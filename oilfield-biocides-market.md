<p><p>The oilfield biocides market plays a critical role in drilling, production, fracturing, and completion processes. In drilling, biocides prevent microbial-induced corrosion and contamination in drilling fluids. During production, they safeguard oil and gas from microbial fouling, which can impair flow and quality. In fracturing, biocides control bacteria that may degrade fracturing fluids or impact reservoir performance. Lastly, in completion, they ensure that well integrity is maintained by inhibiting biofilm formation, enhancing overall operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
