<p><strong>The Pediatric Vitrectomy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pars Plana Vitrectomy</li><li>Anterior Vitrectomy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pediatric vitrectomy market comprises two primary types: Pars Plana Vitrectomy (PPV) and Anterior Vitrectomy. PPV is a minimally invasive procedure that addresses retinal disorders by removing vitreous gel from the eye's posterior segment, often used for conditions like retinal detachment or vitreous hemorrhage in children. Anterior Vitrectomy, on the other hand, focuses on the anterior segment, removing vitreous gel from the front part of the eye, primarily to treat trauma or complications that affect the cornea and lens.</p></p>
