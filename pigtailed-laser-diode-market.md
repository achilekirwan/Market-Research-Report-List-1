<p><p>Pigtailed laser diodes are categorized into two main types: single mode and multimode. Single mode pigtailed laser diodes offer higher precision and are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a narrow optical output. In contrast, multimode pigtailed laser diodes support multiple light modes, resulting in broader optical output, making them suitable for short-distance applications and higher data rates. Both types are essential in telecommunications, fiber optics, and various industrial applications, catering to specific performance and connectivity requirements.</p></p>
