<p><strong>The Precipitation Hardening Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coherency Strain Precipitation Hardening</li><li>Chemical Precipitation Hardening</li><li>Dispersion Precipitation Hardening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precipitation hardening involves strengthening materials through the formation of fine precipitates. In coherency strain precipitation hardening, the precipitates are coherent with the matrix, enhancing strength without disrupting the crystalline structure. Chemical precipitation hardening relies on alloying elements that form precipitates during heat treatment, improving mechanical properties. Dispersion precipitation hardening involves finely dispersed particles within the matrix to resist deformation, thus increasing hardness. Each type uniquely influences material properties, contributing to various industrial applications.</p></p>
