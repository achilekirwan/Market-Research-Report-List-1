<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Premium Tequila Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blanco</li><li>Reposado</li><li>Joven</li><li>Anejo</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The premium tequila market is categorized into several types: </p><p>- **Blanco**: Unaged and bottled right after distillation, offering a pure agave flavor.</p><p>- **Reposado**: Aged in oak barrels for 2 to 12 months, fostering a smooth balance of agave and wood flavors.</p><p>- **Anejo**: Aged for over a year, this type boasts rich, complex flavors and a darker color.</p><p>- **Joven**: A blend of Blanco and aged tequilas, often used for a smoother profile.</p><p>- **Other**: Includes specialty or flavored tequilas, diversifying the market offerings.</p></p>
