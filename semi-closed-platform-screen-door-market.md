<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Control</li><li>Electric Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semi-Closed Platform Screen Door market comprises two primary control types: Pneumatic Control and Electric Control. Pneumatic control systems utilize compressed air for operation, offering reliability and quick response for train station safety. In contrast, Electric control systems employ electric motors to manage door movements, providing precise operation and often integrating advanced technology for enhanced automation. Both types aim to improve passenger safety, reduce noise, and optimize energy efficiency at transit stations, catering to modern urban transportation needs.</p></p>
