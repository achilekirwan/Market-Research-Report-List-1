<p><p>Series compensation systems enhance the efficiency of power transmission by connecting capacitor banks in series with transmission lines, thereby reducing line reactance. The market is divided into three types: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage. Low voltage systems typically serve industrial facilities and commercial buildings. Medium voltage systems are used in regional distribution networks, while high voltage systems cater to long-distance transmission, improving stability and increasing capacity for large-scale power networks and renewable energy integration.</p></p>
