<p><p>The Silicon Carbide Fibre market is primarily divided into two types: Continuous or Long Fibre and Chopped or Short Fibre. In contrast, Chopped or Short Fibre features shorter strands that are easier to handle and integrate into composite materials, suitable for applications requiring flexibility and lightweight properties in various industries.</p></p>
