<p><p>Spray drying equipment is categorized into three market types based on operational complexity and efficiency: </p><p>1. **Single Stage**: Simplest design, ideal for small-scale operations. It evaporates moisture in one continuous step. </p><p>2. **Two-Stage**: Combines two processes, typically pre-drying and final drying, enhancing efficiency and product quality. </p><p>3. **Multi-Stage**: Utilizes several drying stages to increase throughput and optimize energy use, suitable for large-scale industrial applications with high-quality requirements. Each type addresses distinct production needs within various industries.</p></p>
