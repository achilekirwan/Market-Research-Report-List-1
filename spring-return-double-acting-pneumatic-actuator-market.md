<p><p>The Spring Return Double Acting Pneumatic Actuator market comprises three primary types: Rack and Pinion, Scotch Yoke, and Linear actuators. Rack and Pinion actuators convert linear motion into rotational motion, making them suitable for quarter-turn applications. Scotch Yoke actuators utilize a sliding block to produce rotary motion, offering high torque with a compact design. Linear actuators provide direct linear motion, ideal for applications requiring precise positioning. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, enhancing automation and control processes.</p></p>
