<p><p>The semiconductor process tape market is divided into two main types: UV type and non-UV type. UV type tapes are designed for photolithography processes, utilizing ultraviolet light for curing, making them ideal for high-precision applications. Non-UV type tapes, on the other hand, rely on other curing methods, offering versatility across various applications and environments. Both types are essential for ensuring effective adhesion, protection, and ease of handling in semiconductor manufacturing processes, catering to distinct operational needs in the industry.</p></p>
