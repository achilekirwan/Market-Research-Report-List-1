<p><strong>The Water Pressure Booster Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multiple Stages</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water pressure booster pump market is categorized into single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps involve one impeller, making them suitable for low to moderate pressure applications, providing a simple and cost-effective solution for residential needs. In contrast, multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers, enabling them to achieve higher pressures, ideal for commercial or industrial applications requiring significant pressure increases over greater distances. Both types cater to diverse customer requirements, emphasizing efficiency and performance in water distribution systems.</p></p>
