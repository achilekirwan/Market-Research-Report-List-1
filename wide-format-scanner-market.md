<p><p>The wide format scanner market includes two main types: large flatbed scanners and feed-through scanners. Large flatbed scanners are designed for scanning documents and images that are flat and can be placed on a glass surface, accommodating oversized items with high precision. In contrast, feed-through scanners utilize a mechanical feeding system to automatically scan multiple documents in a continuous manner, making them ideal for high-volume tasks. Both types cater to diverse needs in industries such as architecture, engineering, and graphic arts.</p></p>
