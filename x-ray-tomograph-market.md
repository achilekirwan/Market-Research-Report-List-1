<p><p>The X-ray tomograph market is primarily categorized into two types: X-ray scanners and CBCT scanners. They utilize traditional X-ray technology to capture images of internal structures. Conversely, Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) scanners offer three-dimensional imaging, particularly valuable in dentistry and orthopedics, as they enable precise visualization of bone and soft tissues. Both types enhance diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning across different medical fields.</p></p>
